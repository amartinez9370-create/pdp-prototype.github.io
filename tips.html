Dental & Oral Health Tips

Practical tips to maintain strong teeth and healthy gums.


1. Brush and Floss Properly

  • Brush at least twice a day with fluoride toothpaste.
  • Replace your toothbrush every 3 months.
  • Floss daily, especially after sticky rice or sweets.

2. Mind Your Diet

  • Limit sugary snacks and drinks like candies, soft drinks, and sweetened coffee.
  • Eat fiber-rich fruits and vegetables like mangoes, papayas, and leafy greens.
  • Include calcium-rich foods like milk, cheese, yogurt, and sardines.

3. Prevent Gum Disease

  • Watch for red, swollen, or bleeding gums.
  • Use antimicrobial mouthwash and schedule regular dental check-ups.

4. Protect Against Tooth Decay

  • Consider semi-annual fluoride treatments.
  • Avoid frequent snacking on sugary foods to reduce acid attacks.

5. Handle Common Oral Problems Early

  • Rinse with warm salt water for minor toothaches.
  • Use toothpaste for sensitive teeth.
  • Brush your tongue to prevent bad breath.

6. Habits to Avoid

  • Avoid betel nut (nganga) or tobacco products.
  • Do not chew your nails or ice in drinks, which can damage teeth.

7. Regular Dental Check-ups

  • Visit a dentist every 6 months.
  • Bring children early for pediatric dental care.

8. Safe Home Remedies

  • Saltwater rinse for minor gum irritation.
  • Clove oil can temporarily relieve toothache.
  • Avoid traditional remedies like charcoal or ashes that can harm teeth.

9. Oral Health for Children

  • Brush teeth as soon as the first tooth erupts.
  • Avoid bedtime bottles with milk, juice, or sweetened drinks.
  • Offer healthy snacks like fresh fruits instead of candies.

10. Stay Informed

  • Follow reputable Philippine dental websites for tips, news, and preventive programs.
