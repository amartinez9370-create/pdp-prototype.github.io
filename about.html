About Us

Providing quality dental services since 2020, Paciente Dental Pod has been trusted by clients for their dental needs, from General Dentistry to Orthodontic Surgeries.

Established by Dr. Bernadette Paciente, a licensed DMD with 21 years of experience and a wide array of proficiencies in the field of dentistry and certified in advanced orthodontic procedures, Paciente Dental Pod guarantees quality and mindful service tailored for clients of any age.


Accreditations

Paciente Dental Pod is fully accredited by key regulatory and professional institutions in the Philippines to ensure safe, ethical, and quality dental care.


Dr. Bernadette Paciente is a licensed dentist under the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) and an active member of the Philippine Dental Association (PDA), ensuring our practice adheres to nationally recognized standards of ethics and clinical excellence. She is also certified in advanced orthodontic procedures, allowing us to provide specialized care in braces and corrective treatments.


Our clinic is registered with the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR), compliant with the Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR), and officially certified by the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI). These accreditations reflect our commitment to delivering safe, professional, and high-quality dental services to every patient we serve.
